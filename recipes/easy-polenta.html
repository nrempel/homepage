Easy Polenta

Polenta is a great alternative to rice or potatoes for meals. It's very easy to make.

Equipment

  • Wooden spoon
  • Whisk
  • Pot
  • Measuring cups and spoons

Ingredients

  • Corn meal 1 cup
  • Water 4 cups
  • Salt 1 teaspoon
  • Pepper to taste
  • Baking sode a dash
  • Butter 1 tablespoon

Process

  1. Boil 4 cups of water.
  2. Once water is boiling, add 1 teaspoon salt and a dash of baking soda.
  3. Begin carefully stirring the boiling water and, very slowly, add 1 cup of cornmeal while continuing to stir. Stir for 30 seconds after adding cornmeal as it boils then immediately set heat to low and cover pot.
  4. Cook covered for 5 minutes.
  5. Whisk the contents of the pot to get rid of any lumps.
  6. Cook covered for 10 minutes.
  7. Take off heat and stir in 1 tablespoon of butter and pepper to taste.

Serve hot.
