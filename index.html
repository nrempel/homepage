Other Work

There are a handful of other active projects that I've worked on in the past or that I occasionally work on in my spare time:

Bustaprice is a price comparison search engine. Many price comparison search sites have been around for over a decade and lack a modern experience. I wanted to see if I could create a similar price comparison site with a better experience.

Sitemapper is an SEO toolkit that is currently under development. There seems to be a lot of demand for SEO optimization tools and yet the landscape is littered with old and badly designed products. I hope Sitemapper can fill some of the gaps that exist.
